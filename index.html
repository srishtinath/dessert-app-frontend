GOAL: Your mission is to guess the right ingredients AND the right order of ingredients to make the recipe


Instructions

  • Select a recipe from the dropdown below, and click on "Let's start mixing" once you've chosen the recipe you want to work on.
  • Click and drag each ingredient over to the kitchen area.
  • Once you're done guessing, click the "Let's bake it!" button.
  • To clear the space, click on the "Empty bowl" button.
  • If you successfully complete a recipe, you will get skill points. The faster you complete the more skill points you earn!
